Application Overview

The 23MX CGPA Calculator is a specialized tool designed for students of the Master of Computer Applications (MCA) program at PSG College of Technology. This application follows the 23MX regulation and provides accurate CGPA/SGPA calculations based on the institution's grading system.

Built with modern web technologies and featuring a futuristic interface, this calculator helps MCA students track their academic performance efficiently and effectively.
